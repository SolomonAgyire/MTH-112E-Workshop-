Session Objective

Students will master the analysis of rational functions (asymptotes), strengthen foundational skills (function identity, evaluation, domain), find polynomial zeros using synthetic division and theorems, and determine linear equation properties (intercepts, slopes).

Materials Needed

Dry erase markers, Student worksheets, Scratch paper, Calculator, Attendance sheets

üí™ Workout Part 1 - Function Fundamentals (15 mins)

Content to Cover Relation vs. Function (VLT), Function Evaluation, Domain (Rational & Radical)
Collaborative Learning Technique Small Group Problem-Solving (Groups of 3)
Instructions Students work in groups to solve the following problems.

Q4: Find the domain of \(g(x) = \sqrt{3x+9}\)
